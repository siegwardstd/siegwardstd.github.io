Current Project

Desktop
Bouncer

In development — features and visuals may change

Desktop Bouncer is a lightweight desktop overlay game where cute bouncing ball characters live on your screen, react with playful physics, and add a bit of charm to your workspace. The project is still in development, so visuals, features, and content may change over time.

🖥️
Desktop overlay gameplay Runs on top of your workspace, always present, never intrusive.
⚡
Playful physics Real-time bouncing, rolling, and satisfying collision feedback.
🌟
Cute ball companions Ball characters with expressive faces and distinct personalities.
🪶
Lightweight experience Designed to stay out of the way while still being fun to have around.

The base game is planned to be free, with optional cosmetic support content considered for the future.
